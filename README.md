## 👨‍💻 _Executive Summary / About Me_
---

I am a highly-skilled **Server System Network Manager** and **Full-Stack Developer** with a relentless passion for building, securing, and optimizing complex digital ecosystems. I represent the synthesis of **robust software engineering** and **advanced system infrastructure management**.

### **What I Do:**

* 🌐 **Infrastructure Architect:** I design, deploy, and manage resilient and scalable server environments and network topologies. My expertise includes robust system monitoring and virtualization technologies like **ESXi** and **Proxmox**, ensuring high availability and peak performance.
* 💻 **Full-Stack Developer:** I construct comprehensive, end-to-end applications using **Node.js** and **Express.js** for powerful backends, and **Lua**, **HTML5**, and **CSS3** for dynamic, responsive frontends. I believe in clean, efficient, and maintainable code.
* 🛡️ **Cybersecurity Architect:** I don’t just build; I protect. I possess deep knowledge in **Web Application Security** and advanced pentesting techniques. My security arsenal includes **Kali Linux**, **Wireshark**, **Burp Suite**, **Metasploit**, **NMAP**, **Nuclei**, and **Wazuh** for comprehensive vulnerability assessment and ethical hacking operations.

### **My Mission:**

At the core of my work is a commitment to excellence. As the founder of **[Blue Network](https://bluenetwork.tr/)**, I strive to leverage my diverse skill set to provide innovative, secure, and reliable technology solutions that solve real-world problems.
